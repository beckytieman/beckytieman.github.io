Target Audience Description

Website use is for individuals seeking information about current and forecast weather conditions in the Southwest Region of Idaho. These may be residents, travelers driving through the area, temporary visitors, or curious individuals around the globe. Site visitors access the page from various platforms(pc, tablets, smartphone, etc.).

Persona 1

Waylon Marble

  • Age: 52
  • Occupation: High School Athletics Coach
  • Purpose for Site Use: Check to see if weather conditions will affect practices and meets for the high school Cross Country team that he coaches.
  • Site Access: iPad and iPhone.

Persona 2

Alene Hoffman

  • Age: 37
  • Occupation: Highway Patrol Officer at Franklin County Sherrif's Office
  • Purpose for site use: Checking day to day weather conditions for the area that she covers before and during her shift as she expects to travel long distances patrolling the highways in the county.
  • Site Access: Police issued Notebook computer and Samsung Galaxy phone

Scenarios 1-5

  • I am planning to go fishing at Bear Lake this weekend, will there be any rain?
  • My son would like an outdoor birthday party for next week, what can I expect the weather to be like then?
  • I need to find out how cold it will be for my morning run tomorrow.
  • As a truck driver coming through the area I need to see how windy the conditions will be.
  • For a school science project I need to compare real temperature readings for an entire week against the homemade thermometer I made.
