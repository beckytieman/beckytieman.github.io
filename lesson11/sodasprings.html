Screentime Safety Tips

with Angela Morrison

Schools are out on spring break and that means more screen time for our kids. As parents and caretakers we can strive to keep them safe with these simple guidelines. Firstly, don't ban them from internet and devices. Technology can be both rewarding amd fun when used appropriately. Be aware and become edcuated! Explore resources available and take an interest in your childs online presence. Ask questions and keep communication open and positive. Teach your children the basics about online privacy and the importance of age restrictions. Establish boundaries from the beginning. How much time can they spend on various online activites? Will the devices be required to stay in a common area of the house? Will you have tech-free times? Consider creating a family media agreement and allow your children to give input. Monitor your children's online activities and most importanly, set a good example by reviewing your own relationship with technology and your personal devices. For more information and helpful tips, visit KidsHeath.org.
