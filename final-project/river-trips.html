AN ADVENTURE FOR EVERYONE

EXPERIENCE THE SALMON

Start your adventure with one of our three unforgettable trips. Journey down river with us and experience the incredible scenery and exciting rapids of the Salmon. Whether you are experienced or a beginner to rafting, your guide will show you an adventure like no other.

1-DAY SCENIC FLOAT TRIP

6.5 hours

Beginner

A fan favorite trip! Enjoy a relaxing float trip down the Salmon river. Rafts can accomodate groups up to 12 people. You will start the day with a short instructional period from our experienced guides. Afterwards, you will embark on a 12 mile trip down river where you will experience calm and serene waters, perfect for picture taking. We will take a short lunch break(meal included) along the shore line. Time to put the cameras away! Back on the river, we will paddle through some mild rapids as we come to the conclusion of this unforgettable trip. Return transportation can be arranged at extra cost.

$135 per person

2-DAY OVERNIGHT TRIP

2 days/1 night

Beginner/Intermediate

Experience the ultimate overnight trip for groups up to 8 people! Includes all the fun of our popular day trip, but doesn't stop when the sun goes down! Adventure includes lunch & dinner for day one and breakfast for day two. When we arrive to our campsite, feel free to accompany guides on a short hike while the crew sets up camp for you. An amazing stargazing experience awaits you at night fall. After breakfast, you will continue down river where your experienced guides will lead you through some mild & intermediate rapids before our final stop. Ground transportation back to the station is included.

$575 per person: age 12+

X-TREME SALMON ADVENTURE

5 hours

Advanced

Ready to get wet? This trip is for experienced rafters that are looking to test their skill and their nerve! Choose between personal kayaks or six man rafts. Each 8 mile trip will begin with a skill assessment by our experienced guides lasting about 30 minutes. Once we hit the river we will run through a couple calmer sections where you can practice your paddling skills. The remaining portion will be rapids of varying difficulty where you can be sure to get soaked and have a blast!

$95 per person
